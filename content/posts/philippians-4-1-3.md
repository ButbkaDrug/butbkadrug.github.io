### WARNING OF CHAPTER 3 - WORKERS OF EVIL
First obstacle, or a trap to be aware of - is **evil workers**.

> ### Philippians 3:2
> ²Beware of the dogs, beware of the evil workers, beware of the false circumcision;

Essentially, it is people who spread false teachings. They are not openly oppose Christ. Often they disguise like the follower of Christ. But in the reality they work for the other master. Instead of calling them *evil workers* I like to call them  **ministers of the evil one**.

Those who take people away from the true path. Who offer "alternative" ways to heaven, "different gospel". In the day and the time of Paul, it was about salvation by following the law. And this false teaching is still around. As Matt mentioned already, that's what Jehovah witnesses sect is all about. Earning salvation by doing works.

Even though this particular false teaching is very common, unfortunately it's not the only one. There are more. I'm not gonna list them, because it's gonna take a while.

And appropriate response is to **BE AWARE**. We need to see to be able to **discern** who is who. Because even though workers of evil not always openly oppose Christ, they lead people away from him. So in order not get caught by that, we need to **stay alert**, to be **aware**.

**THE FIRST OBSTACLE - is false teachings.**

### WARNING OF CHAPTER 3 - WORDLY CHRISTIANS
Second is the **enemies of the cross**. People who openly leave their life in a manner that is opposes Christ. Paul start to talk about them in a chapter 1, and continue in the chapter 3. Last Sunday we had a brief look at this category of people. Those who may be once followed the Christ, or at least have been coming to the church. But now their lifestyle is far from it.

* They minds set on earthly things.

* They serve their own desires.

* They take pride in things they should be ashamed of.

* An very important note is that **their end is DESTRUCTION**.

Unlike some more liberal opinions in a Christian circles which say: *God will forgive everyone*. This verse clearly shows that it's not the case. If you live a lifestyle of the enemy of Christ, then you clearly heading towards destruction.

And let's not forget that real issue is deeper then the mere actions. Our lifestyle is only a representation of our **MINDSET**! It's like a facade, front end, visible part. It only renders out what is inside of us.

That's why verse 19 Paul says:

> ### Philippians 3:19
> ¹⁹...who have their minds on earthly things.

Or in original language: who have "*EARTHLY PHRONEO*" - if you never heard of PHRONEO, don't worry about it now, we will come back to it later. This is the root issue.

And proper response is not follow their example. Because even if they seem to do fine right now, their end is destruction.

***SECOND OBSTACLE IS EARTHLY MINDSET**


### REAL ONCEs
So we finally land at the third category of people. The real ones. Citizens of heaven. Those who put's their hopes in Christ. Those who serve the Lord. **Imitators of Christ.**

Chapter 3 finishes reminding us that our race is not over yet. With some encouragement:

### Philippians 4:1
 ¹Therefore ... stand firm in the Lord in this way...


Considering that our race isn't over yet, Bible says you must stand firm[STAY'-KO] in the Lord.

Remember when we were studying chapter 1, we talked about [STEY-KO] a lot. We talked that it is a group activity, team sport. That it is like soldiers in the battle holding on their positions under attack. Shoulder to shoulder, relying on one another. With a common goal with a common purpose.

But even when we try our best to do so. Even the most healthy fellowship is not immune to issues. In the verses 2 and 3 we can observe concrete example of such situation:

> ### Philippians 4:2,3
> ²I urge Euodia and I urge Syntyche to live in harmony in the Lord. ³Indeed, true companion, I ask you also, help these women who have shared my struggle in *the cause of* the gospel, together with Clement as well as the rest of my fellow workers, whose names are in the book of life.

Two ladies from the fellowship were having some sort of the conflict. Let's note that both of them were true followers of Christ. They both used to work hard with Paul. They "shared in his struggle" for the sake of the gospel. They were a real deal. Not just anybody.

But somehow they end up in the state of conflict... Which was going on for a while now, since the news had time to reach Paul. Remember that it used to take significant amount of time.

See, it happens to the best of us. Stuff like that happen in healthy churches. In our own fellowship we've had pretty much exactly the same situation multiple times.

Two people having disagreement. Usually  it's not about theology or some sort of the highly spiritual matters. Usually it is something much more close to the ground. Some sort of the personal disagreement and now they can't find a "common ground" anymore.

Unfortunately, in our experience it often it results in both of them stopping attending church groups or they stop coming to the church all together.

### NOT IMMUNE
And it's not about them being weak or immature. None of us immune to this. Potentially anyone  can find himself in similar situation.

In ideal world it's probably never happen. But we are the church of imperfect people. And in our, real world it is possible. We still running our marathon.

Even apostle Paul who had all the reasons to say "It's not about me. I'm more mature then that". Says:

> ### Philippians 3:12 (NLT)
> ¹²I don’t mean to say that I have already achieved these things or that I have already reached perfection. But I press on to possess that perfection...

> ### Philippians 3:13
> ¹³No, dear brothers and sisters, I have not achieved it, but I focus on this one thing: Forgetting the past and looking forward to what lies ahead...

So, don't think it's not about you. Or that you somehow, idk. Due to the extensive experience of walking with Christ. Or due to the high level of spirituality. That somehow you got Immune to this...

No, don't celebrate to early, we are still on the run. We are still awaiting of our Savior.

### WHAT TO DO
So, if find ourselves in such situation, what do we do? Let's look into the verse:

> ### Philippians 4:2
> ²I urge Euodia and I urge Syntyche to live in harmony in the Lord.

This translation says "to live in harmony". But if we will look *under the hood*. Guess what we find there?

**PHRONEO**. - my favorite word in Philippians. Once again Paul repeats what he already said in chapter 2:

> ### Philippians 2:2
> ²make my joy complete by **being of the same mind**[PHRONEO in mind], maintaining the same love, united in spirit, intent on **one purpose**[PHRONEO in perpose].

And he continues in the verse 5:

> ### Philippians 2:5
> ⁵Have **this attitude[PHRONEO]** in yourselves which was also in Christ Jesus...

If we find ourselves in the conflicting situation we should RESET our minds to the right [PHRONEO] - way of looking at things, worldview, mindset. We should adopt PHRONEO of Jesus Christ:

> ### Philippians 2:3,4
> ³Do nothing from selfishness or empty conceit, but with humility consider one another as more important than yourselves;⁴do not *merely* look out for your own personal *interests,* but also for the *interests* of others.

Jesus followed this very principles. And let's be honest, we can't jump any higher that him. (verses 6-11). Adopting right mindset will help us, if not avoid, then to resolve most of our issues.

I have few real life examples when focusing on the wrong thing caused the issue: 

 - **The Coffee War**: Two different churches reported members officially quitting because the church changed its coffee brand from Folgers to a stronger blend.

 - **The Beard Debate**: A church experienced a massive internal dispute regarding the exact "appropriate length" of the pastor's beard.

 - **The Facebook Status Strike:** A woman filed for a split just two months into her marriage simply because her husband had not updated his relationship status to "Married" on social media.

- **The Frozen Movie Divorce**: A woman divorced her husband because he casually mentioned that he didn't think the Disney movie Frozen was all that great. She packed her bags, telling him that if he couldn't appreciate that film, there was something wrong with him as a human being.

- **The Carpet Color Crisis:** A church split down the middle and formed two separate congregations because members could not agree on whether the new sanctuary carpet should be blue or red.

Even though completely real, this ridiculous examples shows how important it is to have a right mindset. The main issues here is not coffee or beard length, Facebook status or color of the carpet. The main issue is **wrong PHRONEO**, wrong mindset.

We should RESET our mindsets to live like a citizen of heaven.

### WHAT IF IT IS DOES NOT WORK
But sometimes two people fail to resolve the issue between themselves. Then verse 3:

> ### Philippians 4:3
> ³Indeed, true companion, I ask you also, help these women who have shared my struggle in *the cause of* the gospel, together with Clement as well as the rest of my fellow workers, whose names are in the book of life.

If you cannot settle your dispute and find the agreement on your own. You SHOULD SEEK and ACCEPT help of the honorable christian leaders.

In this section of the letter Paul asks  a leader of Philippian church(most likely). The language he uses point that he talking to a person equal in authority to himself. Asking to help or literally "to take hold together with one" or "to assist" to resolve this conflict. Literally saying to SEIZE, to ARREST the dispute. Sound funny but I think like it portrays the mood very well.

Bottom line if it is too big to handle between you two. Sometimes church leader, person of authority can assist to push that RESET button.

### HOW IMPORTANT IS IT
We should not be afraid of the conflicts in the church. Because, if we are genuine before one another we will run into things we disagree on. It's totally normal. But we SHOULD RESOLVE our issues.

How important is it? It is important enough to have entire sections of the bible dedicated to this very topic. 

### CONCLUSION
So, we should STEY'-KO together. While we still on the run. And to strive to be like-minded. To have PHRONEO that we find in Christ Jesus. And focus on important stuff that you unite us. And work through the the issues as they arise. We should live like citizens of heaven.
