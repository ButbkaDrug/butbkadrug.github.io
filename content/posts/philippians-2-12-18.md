### Intro
Greetings dear brothers and sisters, we continue study the letter to the church in Philippe. Few words about the context as a reminder to all of us. In order To make sure that we all "*on the same page*" so to speak.

### Context
As was previously said, unlike other letters of Paul, letter to this church does not address any particular issue. It's not like those people were perfect. No, there were couple issues. But all in all they seems to be pretty healthy and mature fellowship of believers. They've been Paul's partner's for many years. And have also supported Paul in his imprisonment, by sending him some financial help as well as one of their members to serve apostle in his needs. Because, even though Paul were allowed not to be put in the dungeon. He were still under arrest. Which makes your life hard if you really think about it. Especially in the first century. But, that for the next times.

### Current
Currently we're at the middle of the chapter two. In the verse 12. We will continue to explore the topic that was started at the end of the chapter one. Few weeks ago we began to talk about **LIVING AS CITIZENS OF HEAVEN**. Or **living the life worthy of the Gospel**. Remember any of that?

### What was covered so far.
It includes a few things. But one of the first that we see is that it's a **team sport**. Not an individual activity. In verse 27 Paul says:

### Philippians 1:27
> ²⁷ ...I will hear about you that you are *standing firm*[steko στήκω](Like a soldiers holding their position under attack) in one spirit, with one mind *striving together*[synathleō συναθλέω](Like a solders who fight shoulder to shoulder, **Holding formation while advancing or resisting an enemy**) for the faith of the gospel;

 ### Together
 Paul uses some military language here. Because remember, it is very likely that good portion of the Philippians were ex-militaries. And they well understood what is Paul trying to say. Think about Roman formations. Like turtle being one of the famous ones. There were quite literally fighting **shoulder to shoulder**. They were in it together. Life of each solder depended on those around him. And each of them had to not only watch for themselves, but also to cover each other backs. Because once formation have open spots, it becomes very venerable.

 So, solder had to be in "one spirit" and "one mind" because their life depended on it.

### Adopt this mentality
And this kind of mentality should be present among the members of the healthy church...

That's what the bible says in the verse 4 of the chapter 2.

### Philippians 2:4
> ⁴do not *merely* look out for your own personal *interests,* but also for the *interests* of others.

Unlike our egocentric society. As a followers of Christ we do not put ourselves into the center around which revolves entire universe. The life of the follower of Christ is not about "*what I want*". No, it's about fulfilling the will of our Father. **TOGETHER**

### FIRST KEY
And the first thing we should embed into our heart today, that we are in it **TOGETHER**. Like, solders in the battle. Where our survival depends on one another. Where if we are not standing for one another, we are effectively defeated. Not like a fan club, which comes together on Sundays to discuss their favorite book. And if someone doesn't show up, well... Who cares, right?

So, **TOGETHER** as a warriors. Looking after one another as if our life depends on it.

### Why we get into this?
Why do I say this? I'll explain.

I put emphasis on this because it comes up every so often in our crowd. And I feel like some of our people might have been disconcert and confused. Especially some of our new comers. So, I felt like I should clear that out.

I don't want us as a church, to fall for the wrong thing. There are a lot of Christians in this world who are after the Hand of Had. Like financial blessings(Prosperity Gospel) and/or after Supernatural, unusual manifestations of the Spirit.

Of course no one  says that. But if you will listen, and observe. Where is the emphasis. What they "*preach*". It is just what it is.

And I'm not saying that they all bed people or something. Many of them are genuine believers. It's just how they've been taught. Maybe it is your case as well.

And before you will get all upset with me, and start throwing rotten tomatoes or even worse get up and leave. Hear me out: I'm not saying that God doesn't bless, or Spirit does not manifest. Stuff happens no doubt. When there is need for that. We can see it in the scriptures. We hear the testimonies of the people. It can edify the body of Christ. And serve as confirmation of the power of the Gospel.

But when manifestation becomes **THE GOAL**. When wealth became **THE GOAL**. When those became a measure of *health* or **maturity** of the church. The measure of *God's presents* in the church. When people say things like:

- If church is not rich, God is not with this Church...
- True believers never struggle financially.

Not the Gospel of Christ...

Or when people say: 

- There are no manifestations of the Spirit in the church. And what they usually mean is the kind I'm want. Something wrong with this Church, with this people, with this pastor\leaders. Church is armature, not spiritual, etc.

And that is simply not true.

People in our crowd does not say it. I might over dramatized it a bit. Or maybe not. But, you can definitely feel the vibe.

And again, just to be clear. I'm not against miracles as such. I'll say more, I always pray for God to move in our fellowship. For the gifts of the Spirit to be at work. For healing of those who needs to be healed. For the word of prophesy, the word of knowledge for those who need em. For God to move any way it **PLEASES TO HIM**.

Let me say it again, in the way it pleases **TO HIM**.

I was always strongly against **enforcing the shape and the form** of how GOD should manifest himself in our midst. It doesn't hurt me much when people talk down on me and stuff. So, I wanted to ask all of us: **Please, stop putting God into the box of your expectations**. And maybe... just maybe... stop watching so much YouTube or whatever type of online content you consume.

Accept that God can work in the ways you do not expect. And it holds true for both. Those you desperate for supernatural. And those who are really skeptical about such movement. We should **HUMBLE** ourselves and to be **OBEDIENT** and then see what God will do. Don't bring down the work of the Holy Spirit by saying "those manifestations are fake". You don't know. I don't know. Maybe they are, may be they aren't. But also, don't bring Holy Spirit down by saying "God doesn't work because I don't see those manifestations". Both are wrong.

### SO THEN
Paul starts this section of the letter from saying "*so then*". Which hints to us that, the following will be logical conclusion of what was said previously. And if you will look up couple verses you will see that Paul talks about Jesus:

That He 1 **Humbled** himself, then **humbled** himself even more, by being **OBEDIENT** to extreme degree. And as a result of **THAT**, God highly exalted Him. Paul lists all the beautiful blessings are rewards which God bestowed on Jesus for his **HUMILITY** and **OBEDIENCE**.

### CITIZENS OF HEAVEN
This two are the core characteristics of a true **CITIZEN OF HEAVEN**. You can't tell citizens of heaven by the color of their skin. You not gonna tell them by the shape of their eyes. Nor by their fitness level. You can't tell by their appearance. 

But this two, **HUMILITY** and **OBEDIENCE** are very strong indicators. God values this characteristics so very much. Every citizen of heaven should have them.

It's like sports. Like heavy lifting for example. I'm not too much of the lifter as you can tell. But, I know the thing or two. You don't do it once and you good for life... No, you have to continue to do it. And more you do it, better you at it.

And Paul reminds us of that, he says:

### PRESENCE vs ABSENCE
> ...just as you have always obeyed, not as in my presence only, but now much more in my absence, work out your own salvation with fear and trembling

That's a mouthful sentence. Let's try to break it down. 

### DO NOT STOP
Paul is saying do not stop pursuing relationships with God. You would continue to ACTIVELY LISTEN/GIVE EAR to what is God saying to you. He says you've been obedient when I was with you, it's even more important now, when I'm away.

Unfortunately in reality is often hard to do. It's easier to do the right thing when your leader/mentor/pastor is near. And require fare bit of maturity to continue to be the same way when you on your own.
